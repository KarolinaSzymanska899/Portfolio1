ABOUT ME

Why am I passionate about Frontend Development?

The main reason is that front-end development satisfies my need for creativity. It enables me to easily put my ideas into digital form and get results back really quick. I still remember the first time I created an HTML page and opened it in the browser. A whole new world of possibilities appeared before my eyes.

Which attributes do help me in Frontend Development?

I am involved in Web Development, independent, like challenges both in projects and private life. These make me take a step further. In addition to this, I like people, troubleshooting and riddles.

Why do I find User Centered Design important?

When I design a new product, it's always essential to me to understand who and how people will use it. Satisfying user's needs and wants becomes a priority, and every design decision is evaluated in the context of whether it delivers value to the users.
